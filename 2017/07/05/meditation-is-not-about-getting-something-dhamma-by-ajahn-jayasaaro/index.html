Meditation is not about getting something ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

July 05, 2017
Translation(s): ไทย 
Edit on Github

Meditation is not about getting something. It's about learning how to face up to the present moment in the wisest way possible way. One day you may be learning about peaceful states of mind, and on another day you may be learning about unpeaceful states of mind. It's certainly more enjoyable to learn about peaceful states of mind, but not necessarily any more beneficial. Some people say they are too agitated to meditate. That is like saying that you are too sick to take medicine

Ajahn Jayasāro
