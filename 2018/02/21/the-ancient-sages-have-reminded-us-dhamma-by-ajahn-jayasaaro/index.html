The ancient sages have reminded us ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

February 21, 2018
Translation(s): ไทย 
Edit on Github

The ancient sages have reminded us to look at the moon, not the finger pointing to the moon. They mean that we should not allow ourselves to become obsessed with words and terminology, but focus on the truth that the words refer to.

And it is not only words that lead us to the Dhamma. As we learn how to let go of seeing everything that happens in terms of ‘me’ or ‘mine’, more and more of our daily experiences of pain and pleasure, gain and loss are revealed as being also like fingers pointing to the moon.

Ajahn Jayasāro
