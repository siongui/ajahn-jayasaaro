Meditation is like rubbing two sticks ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

May 06, 2015
Edit on Github

Meditation is like rubbing two sticks together to make fire. You need a lot of patience to be successful, and you need consistency and continuity. Perhaps you start with great enthusiasm, but that won't last. If when you start to feel tired or bored or discouraged, please understand that you can't just stop for a while, for a few days or weeks, and then just carry on. The two sticks will be cold and you will have to start again.

So even if you only do a little every day, never mind. What is important is that you don't stop.

Ajahn Jayasāro
