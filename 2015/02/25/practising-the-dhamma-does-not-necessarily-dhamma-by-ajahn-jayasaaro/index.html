Practising the Dhamma does not necessarily ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

February 25, 2015
Edit on Github

Practising the Dhamma does not necessarily mean that the path of life becomes smooth. What does become smooth is our journey along the path.

Imagine a rutted mountain road full of potholes. Now imagine the difference between travelling along that road in an old truck with no suspension and in an air-conditioned S.U.V. It is the vehicle that matters; and the most important vehicle we have is our mind

Ajahn Jayasāro
