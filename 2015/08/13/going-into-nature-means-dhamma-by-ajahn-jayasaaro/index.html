Going into nature means ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

August 13, 2015
Edit on Github

Going into nature means entering a world that has its own rules, rules that make no compromises with your desires. You cannot bargain with nature. You cannot bully it. Nature is unmoved by your tears. If you are to survive in nature you must respect it. You must learn its ways and adapt to it.

When you are respectful and humble, patient, alert and intelligent then nature will bless you with joy and understanding. This is true of both the world around us, and the world that we discover when we close our eyes in meditation.

Ajahn Jayasāro
