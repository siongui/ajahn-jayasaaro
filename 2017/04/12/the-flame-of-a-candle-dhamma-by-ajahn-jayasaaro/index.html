The flame of a candle ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

April 12, 2017
Translation(s): ไทย 
Edit on Github

The flame of a candle exposed to the wind is unsteady. It is not possible to use its light to work by

The mind is like a candle flame. Only when it is protected from the winds of agitated thought and negative emotion, does it provide a light bright and still enough to enable the work of wisdom

Ajahn Jayasāro
