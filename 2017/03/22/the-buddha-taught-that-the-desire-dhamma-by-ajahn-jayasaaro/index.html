The Buddha taught that the desire ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

March 22, 2017
Translation(s): ไทย 
Edit on Github

The Buddha taught that the desire to boast or manipulate people's perceptions of us is a mental defilement. Today, social media has given a huge boost to boasting. Referring to boasting as 'sharing' does not necessarily mean that it is no longer boasting. Nor does adding a humble comment to a boast.

Intention is all. If you feel a need to edit a photograph of yourself to take away skin blemishes, to make you look thinner or more attractive, investigate the reason why.

Mindfulness does not just mean being in the present moment; it means being aware of whether our intentions are wholesome or unwholesome. Then making the effort to abandon the unwholesome and cultivate the wholesome.

Ajahn Jayasāro
