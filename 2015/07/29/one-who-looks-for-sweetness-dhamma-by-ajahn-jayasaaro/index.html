One who looks for sweetness ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

July 29, 2015
Edit on Github

One who looks for sweetness in a sack of chillies will be disappointed. One who looks for a dropped key in a place that is easy to search, rather than where it was lost, will not find it.

Impermanent pleasures can never provide the lasting happiness that our heart desires. Only by learning how to look closely at our body and mind will we find the key to liberation

Ajahn Jayasāro
