The good things in a garden ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

March 25, 2015
Edit on Github

The good things in a garden grow well when the gardener works hard to keep the garden free from weeds. Our heart is like a garden. Virtuous qualities will grow well and blossom when we work hard to keep it free from the weeds of defilement.

Ajahn Jayasāro
