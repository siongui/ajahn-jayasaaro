Observe the joy of learning ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

May 27, 2015
Edit on Github

Observe the joy of learning and cultivate it. Encourage those around you - teach them when you can - to observe the joy of learning and to cultivate it. This joy of learning, of growing and of flourishing as a human being, will gradually wash away our joy in things that harm our bodies and damage our hearts.

Ajahn Jayasāro
