Awake and aware in the present moment ... - Dhamma by Ajahn Jayasāro

March 18, 2015
Edit on Github

Awake and aware in the present moment - this is our home base. Whether we are in our house, at work or even travelling abroad far from our loved ones and friends, if we can access this bright clear awareness we feel instantly at home.

Ajahn Jayasāro
